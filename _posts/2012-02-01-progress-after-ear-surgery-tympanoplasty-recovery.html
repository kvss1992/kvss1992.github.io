Healing and recovery from ear surgery. tags: - Ear - Hearing - Life Events - Surgery - Tympanoplasty status: publish type: post published: true meta: title: Progress and Post Operative period after Tympanoplasty keywords: Healing, Ear Surgery, Tympanoplasty, Tympanoplasty Healing, Recovery readmore: read more on my progress --- Its been over 20 days since my ear surgery and I am happy to say that I am recovering well. Its not as quick as I had anticipated however my doctor thinks its satisfactory. Healing is a gradual process or so, I have learned.

The Good stuff

  • I have resumed my normal routine and have joined college again.
  • I am able to work without any discomfort or issues.
  • Pain & swelling post surgery have all subsided and my ear looks just fine
  • I should be able to hear again from operated ear in about 45 days time
  • The cotton swabs and bandages are all off.
  • The sutures have healed and there's no scar! yay!

The Bad stuff

  • Ear discharge still persists and needs cleaning up every few hours or so
  • Its a little annoying to keep cleaning up your ear but I guess that's part of the recovery process too.
  • Although not being able to hear from one side is not much of an issue, it needs a little getting used to in college where you need to listen to friends,folks and sometimes even the professors.
In all I am happy with my progress. I did not experience any major issues and it all fell in place. Each day was an improvement and made me feel better. Although, I do have to admit the first week was a little uncomfortable due to the discomfort and mild pain from time to time. I am hoping to be able to hear again in the next few weeks. All the best to me!
